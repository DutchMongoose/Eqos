# Knowing Keepers

> *"The ones who amass knowledge and share it with those capable."*

The Keepers claim an origin tied to the god of knowledge, [[Ioun]], who after the [[The Divergence|Divergence]] tasked them with finding truths and solutions. Colloquially known as **the Academy** in [[Varrens]].

---

## Public Goals
- Collect hidden knowledge and artifacts
- Study ruins of the Divergence and the knowledge hidden within
- Counteract rising rivals

## Hidden Goals
- Subdue the population through selective knowledge sharing
- Safeguard the [[World Anchors|world anchors]]
- Spread influence throughout the lands

## Leadership — The Illustrious Five

### [[The Eye]]
The head of the Five. A reclusive and mysterious figure that many believe to have been around as long as anyone can remember. See The Eye for full details.

### Faethana *(#2)*
Appears as a young female elf — in reality a **hag** from the [[Feywild]]. Left the [[Seelie Court]] to expand her powers away from prying eyes. She oversees the **[[Chain Breakers]]**, a covert group of ~30 assassin-mages, many hidden in plain sight in neighbouring empires. Deception and shades are her core skills; she can project a charming atmosphere to influence her targets.

### Golner *(#3)*
A male **storm giant** from the cloud nation of [[Reylor]]. Blue-grayish skin, stands roughly 20 feet tall. Wears an almost ritual arrangement of overlapping leather straps with dozens of metal rings braided throughout his long beard, and thick metal bands on his arms — each of which stores a spell for later use, making him a dangerous opponent when confronted directly. Originally the lorekeeper for his nation; now responsible for **excavations and artifact gathering**.

### Venetta *(#4)*
An old female human with a long singular braid of blond hair. Carries a multitude of lenses and apparatuses for reading, distant observation, and field use. Responsible for **books and general inventory** for the Academy, organized in a massive binder she carries on her back.

### Brilyn *(#5)*
An older elven woman. Responsible for **classes, recruitment, and the Academy** in general.

## The Academy (Varrens HQ)

Located in the capital Varrens, the Academy maintains a University open to students of all walks of life.

### Layout
The main building is square-shaped with two larger structures anchoring it.

- **Courtyard (inner square)** — Benches, fountains, and shallow ponds. The outer ring holds unorganized library shelves open to all.
- **Front building** — Entrance with a secretary checking credentials; small public library inside.
- **Back building** — The true headquarters. Multiple floors opening onto a grand hall lit by a **stained glass roof**. The hall ends in a semi-circle glass window overlooking a nature illusion.

### The Obsidian Throne
At the base of the back hall's window sits an **obsidian throne** bearing a figure of a bearded man with a scythe and book. With proper credentials, the figure rises to reveal a hidden tunnel within the throne. Those who enter must make a **WIS saving throw** or feel as though someone is peering over their shoulder.

### The Secret Chamber
Beyond the throne: a one-story brick room lit by torches. Dark-grayish figures with **white masks** (each uniquely marked — dots, lines, slashes) wander the room, opening and replacing books endlessly. These are the **Guardians**, discovered alongside the throne. They protect a portion of the Keepers' most secret information.

## The Chain Breakers

> [!warning] Classified
> Their very existence is officially dismissed as a bedtime tale. Students who discuss them are temporarily banned from the Academy.

A covert operations group: a hybrid of mages and rogues with access to **ancient magics predating the Divergence**. Each member bears **runes of power** tattooed (more accurately, branded, with object insertion into the skin) on their arms. These tattoos empower spells and grant access to various ancient magics when fully trained.

*See also: [[Rhazi]] — former Chain Breaker recruit who defected to [[The Fallen]].*

# The Fallen

> *"The disenfranchised and the broken — those abandoned by the system — all fighting for a world where they can live a better life."*

Persuaded and seduced by the **[[Banished Ones|banished gods]]**, the Fallen seek to undo the works of the [[Knowing Keepers]] — specifically, the destruction of the [[World Anchors|world anchors]] that keep the planes of existence separate.

---

## Leadership — The Fated Siblings

### [[Rhazi]]
*See Rhazi for full profile.*
A former [[Chain Breakers|Chain Breaker]] recruit who defected. Pale woman with long dark hair, deep dark eyes, and a double-line tattoo running from her mouth down her neck. Has self-inflicted scars where she erased her chainbreaker runes. Trained in magic — some spells altered and powered by the Banished Ones. Gained a **third eye** on her forehead (hideable at will) that can reflect spells and emit a psychic burst.

Travels with **[[Rha'zoc]]** — a 15-foot crow creature, one of the last of its kind from ancient times. Can melt into shadows and assume a humanoid form by hiding in a cloak, its beak visible like a plague mask.

### [[Nherdur]]
*See Nherdur for full profile.*
A gaunt, dark figure — stoic and pessimistic. Former [[Black Hand]] member, now swordsman and hemocraft practitioner. Carries twin swords with special grooves for hemocraftic rituals; by slashing his blades along his arms they ignite in varying colors of flame.

## Sub-factions

### The Starborn
Forces provided by the Banished Ones — souls transported to the **Far Realms** during the [[The Divergence|Divergence]]. Over the years their minds and bodies were corrupted: weaker minds devolved into **lesser star spawn** (grunts); stronger ones retained conscience and warped magics over mind and space.

Once enough world anchors are destroyed, they will arrive on the material plane via **meteorite** — manifesting as a dark mass of worms that infect dead hosts, assuming their form while retaining the ability to dissolve into a formless mass. They will then travel to major cities, preaching the truth of the Divergence to the masses.

### The Ashen Faithful
Just before [[Fior]] collapsed, a seer sensed doom and led survivors down the **[[Sankrit]]** into the underdark. Over generations they adapted to darkness — losing skin pigment and gaining enhanced darkvision. A civilized society now exists just beneath the Sankrit opening. During the conflict of reunification they emerged and joined the Fallen in [[Varrens]].

### The Insects of Disease
A lone agent, twisted by the god formerly known as **Myrnes**. His body became a vessel for a foul disease — eyes like a fly, hives as skin, bones and joints warped beyond function. He can barely move, living in a moisture-filled undercity abode swarming with **ant-mosquito hybrid insects**.

The insects burrow into targets, inject anesthetic acid, and lay an egg. The larva eats its way to a vital organ (typically the heart), causing slow death via fever, hallucinations, and loss of awareness. Diseased bodies are thrown into the Sankrit — inadvertently preventing discovery of the cause.

# The Eye — Aexidor

> *"A random passerby would find a small hunter's cabin."*

The supreme leader of the [[Knowing Keepers]]. To the outside world, a recluse. In truth, one of the most powerful beings on Eqos.

---

## True Nature
Aexidor is an **ancient green dragon**, disguised as an androgynous, bald human named **Aexi**. Their survival since the [[History Overview#Age of Flame|time of dragon empires]] is itself a mark of extraordinary power. Sensing the fall of dragonkind, they hid — waiting for a more opportune time.

## Location — The Cabin
Aexidor resides in an unassuming hunter's cabin deep in the **[[Grey Woods]]**, just north-east past the [[Sea of Discovery]].

The cabin is genuine on the surface. But a curtain leading to the "bedroom" opens into something else entirely.

## The Demiplane
Beyond the curtain lies a **private demiplane**: a vast circular library rising impossibly high, marble pillars supporting floor after floor with no clear way of climbing, and a large opening at the center.

At its heart: the **main [[World Anchors|world anchor]]** — a singular twisting tree with a faint greenish glow and red leaves. Approaching the tree causes smaller branch extensions to reach out, forming stairs that guide visitors to the intended floor.

> [!danger] Primary Objective
> The protection of the main world anchor is the Eye's core hidden priority within the Keepers.

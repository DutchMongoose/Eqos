# Minor Factions of Rothas

---

## The Golden Carp
The **traders guild** of [[Varrens]]. Determines pricing of goods to ensure fair trade and prevent undercutting. Also governs what goods can legally be sold under [[Rothas]] law.

For everything outside the law, they maintain an **ever-updating agreement with the [[Black Hand]]**: protection for their wares in exchange for compensation and first pick at special goods.

---

## Clerical Orders
While the worship of gods in Varrens is largely centralized at the **Temple of Enlightenment**, all religions maintain their own establishments and communities. Each order has a separate building exclusive to devotees, themed around their deity's aspect.

> [!info] Nature Shrines
> Clerical houses dedicated to spirits of the land and aspects of nature are often mistaken for gardens by the public — their exteriors are typically covered entirely in plants of all sorts.

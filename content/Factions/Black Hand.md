# Black Hand

> *"Need a shady job done or something not technically legal delivered?"*

The underbelly of [[Rothas]], with a hand in almost every pot. Based primarily in the **undercity of [[Varrens]]**, where they have served as judge and executioner since the **Cavern Riots of 824** — after the city guard withdrew from the cave system. The Varrens guard has attempted several times to retake the caverns, but the tunnels are too extensive and the population too disgruntled.

---

## Approach
The Hand usually finds out about outsiders seeking them and sends a **Speaker** to make contact. In rare cases, they can be approached at **[X]** — a dive bar next to the undercity lake.

## Ideal Member Traits
Craftiness, perceptive, sleight of hand.

## Notable Members

### [[The Mask]] — Gearta
A **doppelganger** who originally built her own crew, drew the Hand's attention, and after refusing to submit — sparked a bloody internal faction war. The result was a new Mask and a full restructuring of the Hand. Has led the Hand for centuries.

### The Fist
An elite group dispatched for high-value targets. They can melt into shadows, traveling almost tracelessly.

### The Speakers
The Hand's mouthpieces — handlers for missions and client liaison. Most possess some form of **disguise-based magic**, necessary since they must sometimes venture above ground.

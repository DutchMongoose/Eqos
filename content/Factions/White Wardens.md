# White Wardens

> *"The sword and shield of Rothas."*

The White Wardens form the de facto army of [[Rothas]], renowned for their infantry combat effectiveness. Unlike nations such as [[Ciria]] that specialize in war magics, the Wardens rely purely on the physical — grounded in a deep sense of **justice and camaraderie**.

Each squad is led by a sergeant capable of making quick in-combat decisions, enabling great adaptability and rapid strategy shifts. Historic victories still empower the faction, though cracks are forming: excess pride and overextension are leading to unruliness and tardiness.

## Leadership

### [[Thrurdir Redfist]] — The Red Storm *(Kedar)*
The current leader. A ginger dwarf with arms like logs. See Thrurdir Redfist.

### The Silvers — [[Norel]] & [[Qilay]] *(Si-Kedars)*
Two half-celestial women — half-orc and half-elf respectively. In combat they unfurl their scimitars and **silvery wings**, raining death on enemies with an ever-present grimace. Discovered as celestial-born babies during an investigation into an angelic cult; placed in Warden care after their penchant for mischief became apparent.

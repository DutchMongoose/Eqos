# Rha'zoc

A lone crow creature — one of the last survivors of an ancient species. Companion and mount of [[Rhazi]].

## Appearance
Stands **15 feet tall** with a wingspan to match. In the open, its true form is unmistakable. In public, it assumes a humanoid disguise: hiding within a large cloak, only the tip of its beak visible like a plague mask. Looking into the hood of the cloak reveals two glinting eyes in the far distance — making clear the cloak is somehow *bigger on the inside*.

## Abilities
- Can **melt into shadows** and lie in wait undetected
- Assumes a convincing humanoid silhouette via the cloak disguise

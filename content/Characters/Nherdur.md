# Nherdur

Co-leader of [[The Fallen]] alongside his sister [[Rhazi]]. A stoic swordsman and practitioner of hemocraft.

---

## Appearance
A gaunt, dark figure with dark hair to his shoulders. Two thin engraved swords at his side. A single-line tattoo runs below each eye and mouth. Scores of scars line his arms and legs, gained early in his studies. Wears basic travel leathers with a hood — fairly inconspicuous, for as far as his appearance allows.

## Personality
More pessimistic and stoic than his sister. Capable of magic but prefers swordsmanship and speed.

## Weapons — Hemocraftic Twin Swords
His twin swords have special **grooves for hemocraftic rituals**, enhancing their properties. By slashing the blades along his own arms, they **ignite in varying colors of flame**. Hemocraft was hidden from the public — Nherdur discovered hidden tomes through the guidance of **Narja**.

---

## Background
Left to survive alone in the undercity after his father's execution. Joined the [[Black Hand]] for money and quickly gained skill with the blade. One night in the dark, *an eye opened* — a voice echoed in his mind, revealing the truths hidden beneath the [[Knowing Keepers]]' version of history. The god formerly known as **Narja** slowly showed him the folds keeping the Keepers' tale from falling apart, making him bitter and setting him on his current path.

Eventually reunited with his sister Rhazi, and together they began working toward dismantling the [[World Anchors]].

# Rhazi

Co-leader of [[The Fallen]] alongside her brother [[Nherdur]]. A former [[Chain Breakers|Chain Breaker]] recruit who turned against the [[Knowing Keepers]].

---

## Appearance
A pale woman with long dark hair and deep dark eyes. A double-line tattoo runs from her mouth down her neck. Her arms bear several **self-inflicted scars** where she erased the start of her chainbreaker runes. She has a **third eye** on her forehead — gained in service to the [[Banished Ones]] — which she can hide at will.

## Abilities
- Trained in magic, with some spells altered and powered by the Banished Ones
- Her third eye can **reflect spells** directed at her and **emit a psychic burst**

## Companion — [[Rha'zoc]]
A lone crow creature, one of the few survivors of an ancient species. Stands **15 feet tall** with a wingspan to match. Can melt into shadows and lie in wait. In public, assumes a humanoid form by hiding within a cloak — only the tip of its beak visible, like a plague mask. Looking into the hood reveals two glinting eyes in the far distance, making it clear the cloak is *bigger on the inside*.

---

## Background
Grew up in the undercity of [[Varrens]] with her brother Nherdur after the execution of their father at the **Tower of Feathers**. Showing magical talent early, she was recruited by the Knowing Keepers and groomed for the Chain Breakers. As assignments grew increasingly extreme, she sought out her brother — and together they resolved to change the world, hiding in old temples of the Banished Ones and forgotten gods.

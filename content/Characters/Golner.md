# Golner

Third of the [[Knowing Keepers|Illustrious Five]]. A male **storm giant** from the cloud nation of [[Reylor]].

## Appearance
Blue-grayish skin, stands roughly **20 feet tall**. Wears an almost ritual arrangement of overlapping leather straps with dozens of metal rings braided throughout his long beard, and thick metal bands on his arms — **each band stores a spell for later use**, making him a dangerous opponent when confronted directly.

## Role
Responsible for **excavations and artifact gathering** for the Academy. Originally served as the lorekeeper for [[Reylor]] before departing to join the Keepers.

# The Mask — Gearta

Supreme leader of the [[Black Hand]]. A **doppelganger** who has led the Hand for centuries.

## History
Originally built her own crew, which drew the attention of the Black Hand. After refusing to submit to their authority, she sparked a **bloody internal faction war** — the result being a new Mask, a full restructuring of the Hand, and Gearta at its head.

## Nature
As a doppelganger, Gearta can assume any form. Her true appearance is unknown even to most of her lieutenants. She is one of the children of [[Kalryn]] — though whether she serves his current agenda is unclear.

# Faethana

Second of the [[Knowing Keepers|Illustrious Five]]. Appears as a young female elf — in reality a **hag** from the [[Feywild]] who left the [[Seelie Court]] to expand her powers away from prying eyes.

## Abilities
- Deception and shadow magic are her core skills
- Can project a **charming atmosphere** to influence targets around her

## Role
Oversees the [[Chain Breakers]] — the Keepers' covert assassin-mage division of ~30 operatives, many embedded in neighbouring empires.

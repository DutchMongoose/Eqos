# Thrurdir Redfist — The Red Storm

A ginger dwarf with arms like logs. Leader of the [[White Wardens]] and a living legend of Rothas warfare.

---

## Appearance & Combat Style
Prefers **light armor** for surprising speed and dexterity — unusual for a dwarf of his stature. Wields an enormous **silver-encrusted maul**, decorated with engravings of his feats. Charges into battle with seemingly no sense of self-preservation, leaving a trail of broken bones behind. Survivors describe seeing *a red storm* sweep across the battlefield.

## Notable Feats
- Crushed the rebellion of the **Tarnished Oaks**
- Won an arm wrestling contest against a tribe of **hill giants**
- Established the current squad structure of the Wardens

## Background
Born in the mountains of [[Gira]], just after the nation joined [[Rothas]] following the war of expansion. Drawn to Rothas's apparent might from an early age, he left home to adventure and enrolled in the Wardens — rising quickly through the ranks to Si-Kedar under Kedar **Farkir**. When Farkir unexpectedly perished in an ambush, Thrurdir stepped up as Kedar and has held the position since.

## Present Day
Spends his time overseeing recruit training in [[Tharium]] and retelling his feats — a habit contributing to the organizational decay of the Wardens.

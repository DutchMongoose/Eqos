# The Caves of the Departed

The final resting place for all souls. A dark, cave tunnel system with hundreds of faces covering the walls.

## How It Works
When a soul arrives, it restlessly roams the caves. Once it finds peace with its new reality, it settles along a wall — leaving only a faint outline of its face, reliving its life and slowly sinking into the stone. **A soul is fully lost to the Caves when its name is forgotten by the living.**

## Exploration
Wandering here means getting lost almost instantly, amid near-constant whispering — prayers, requests for forgiveness, sleep talk. Visitors risk losing their sense of self, bathing in the memories of others. Caves grow narrow and wide based on the impact of nearby souls — massive galleries may be dedicated to singular powerful beings.

Death makes no distinction between good and evil; powerful souls of any alignment can persist here for ages. The [[Raven Queen]] wanders her domain — one may come eye to eye with her.

# The Feywild

Home of the fey, good and evil alike. All climates from the material plane exist here, but taken to extremes — a large mountain range becomes one that reaches the sky indefinitely; a calm river becomes a treacherous current that drowns its victims.

## Sky
Adorned by the **Dusk Star** — a sun that shines permanently low on the horizon, creating eternal dusk. Several moons reflect its light, creating faux suns.

## The Seelie & Unseelie Courts
Governed by archfey — powerful godlike beings, each unique in personality and governance.

| Court | Season | Regent | Character |
|-------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Seelie Court** | Summer | Queen Titania | Charming, proud, beautiful — angered by those of greater beauty approaching the Court |
| **Unseelie Court** | Winter | Nintra, the Gloam | Sinister, cold — the Court seems almost frozen, flowers pale and frost-encased |

The two courts are considered enemies, their aims and methods opposed — yet the balance between them cannot be disturbed, as such is nature.

The two castles sit on opposite banks of **The Ticking Waters**. At mid-season, when one court yields to the other, **The Shifting Soiree** is held on the water. Enormous **floating gears** drift above the lake's surface, constantly in motion — symbolising time and progress. On the northern shore, **Otaral** sits with a **dimension portal** in its main square. The portal is defended by **portal guardians**, without whom it cannot function. The city incorporates architecture and techniques drawn from other realms, though these fail to work further from the portal.

## Elf Origins
Feywild history centres heavily on the origins of the elves. In the beginning there was only **total darkness** — in this world the **shadow elves** were born under the primordial goddess **Mother Dark**. The relationship soured when Mother Dark bonded with **Father Light** to bring light to the world. Shadow elves consider this rejection by their mother a great turning point in their history.

Father Light had his own children — the **sun elves** — who remain hostile to shadow elves to this day. From the bond between Mother Dark and Father Light came the **grey elves**, who became the everyday elves and spread across both the Feywild and Eqos.

Some elves, now called **Eladrin**, developed a deep bond with the chaos of the Feywild — forming a direct connection to a specific season, shaping their appearance and personality accordingly.

## Factions
All fey hold some allegiance to one or more archfey, though these alliances shift as easily as the seasons. The most powerful are the queens of the Seelie and Unseelie Courts.

| Faction | Notes |
|---|---|
| **The Howling Legion** | Werewolf-like group; isolated, rarely conflicts with others |
| **The Sages of the Winds** | Four ancient hermit sages of great wisdom; their dwellings regularly change location |
| **The Rust Baron** | Lord of metal constructs; has built his court from mechanical beings |
| **The Rat Guild** | Thieves guild equivalent |
| **The Lord of the Hunt** | Responsible for the forests; leads the Wild Hunt |
| **The Toad Regent** | A princess waiting for her prince |
| **The Snow Queen** | Has a considerable number of giants in her court |
| **Baba Yaga** | Powerful hag who wants nothing to do with the courts; lives in a hut on chicken legs |

# The Abyss

Where devils abide by laws and contracts, demons from the Abyss are **chaos embodied**. Shadows growing from ravines into nightmarish forms; thirsting for blood and violence. The Blood War with the Hells keeps their Legions from running rampant through the material plane. The deeper one travels down the Layers, the more chaotic the denizens. Each layer is claimed by a demon lord powerful enough to control rivals and guide armies.

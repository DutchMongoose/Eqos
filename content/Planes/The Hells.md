# The Nine Hells

Home of devils — conniving beings who trick desperate souls into bad contracts. Payment usually involves souls in some form. Each of the seven layers is governed by an archdevil.

## Society
Devil ranks are linear; each promotion is a literal physical transformation, from formless soul mass up through the ranks to archdevil. Even condemned souls can rise by performance or betrayal. Devils are in eternal conflict with the **[[The Abyss|Legions of the Abyss]]** — the **Blood War** — which channels their chaos productively.

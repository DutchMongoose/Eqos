# Far Realms

*"Fractured, broken, maddening, repeating."*

A collection of shards of space, each ranging from several feet to many miles, varying in all dimensions. The logic of space here can drive one to madness — shards can be both directly connected and not, and able to connect to themselves. Looking up might give a view of another place, or show yourself scattered throughout many shards.

## Environment
The general space is colored a **deep purple** with occasional thunderstorms, giving the impression of outer space. Fragments of land ripped from other dimensions float about — buildings held aloft in strange orientations, towers tilted impossibly, missing their bases.

> [!warning] Dreams Become Reality
> Should one sleep in the Far Realms, be careful what you dream of — dreams manifest here.

## Denizens
All inhabitants have been consumed and corrupted since being plucked from their home. The Far Realms heightens perception and susceptibility to the power of the mind. Creatures take on all forms.

- **[[The Starborn]]** — corrupted souls from the [[The Divergence|Divergence]], now servants of the [[Banished Ones]]
- **[[Sanci]]** — the Devourer, roaming as a 100-ft diameter snake
- **Dream Serpents** — massive snakes spanning several shards at once, observed but never aggressive — though their size suggests immense strength

# Age of Divinity

Tribes merged and grew, forming humanoid empires. Progress and arcane knowledge reached their height. Almost every major city contained permanent portals to other dimensions.

Many powerful creatures roamed Eqos, some attracting followers as small cults began deifying them. Dragons were deemed extinct.

This era ended with the [[The Divergence|Divergence]].

# The Divergence — Year 0 PD

An explosion of light and a rupture of terrain tore apart the singular continent of Eqos. The capital of [[Fior]] was reduced to rubble, its population destroyed.

Every nation interprets the cause differently:
- **[[Phokar]]** — Bahamut engulfed the world in flames and flew into the sun
- **[[Ciria]]** — Birth of a new star that broke through the ground
- **Various tribes** — Day of Retribution, ancient spirits purifying the world
- **[[Knowing Keepers]]** — The Benevolent Gods warred with and sealed the [[Banished Ones]]
- **[[The Fallen]]** — All gods were sealed away equally

> [!warning] Records detailing events near Fior were lost in the Divergence itself.

# Age of Flame

Powerful scaled creatures rose with a greed unknown to the world's inhabitants. Each dragon emperor subjugated creatures and slowly transformed them to serve their interests.

Over centuries, dragon empires warred with each other. Many were eventually overthrown or abandoned their territories.

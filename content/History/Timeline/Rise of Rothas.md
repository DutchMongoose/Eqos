# Rise of Rothas — 3rd Century PD

[[House Rothuss]] traveled to the site of the Divergence, built a fortress from the ruins, and established an outpost at the **Gates** — the only feasible land entry into the area.

Key events of this century:
- Rothas established as a trade nation
- [[Knowing Keepers]] and [[White Wardens]] rise to prominence
- Rothas absorbs the [[Endless Plains]]
- War with [[Kasev]] — fortress [[Mekti]] overwhelmed by Warden strategies

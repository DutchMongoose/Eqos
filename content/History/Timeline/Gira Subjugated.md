# Gira Subjugated — 6th Century PD

[[Gira]] is driven back and becomes a vassal state of [[Rothas]].

The southern campaign was extended due to difficult mountain terrain, eventually resolved by a flanking force of [[Knowing Keepers|Keeper]] mages. Skirmishes with [[Ciria]] continued with no real gain — an uneasy trade partnership formed instead.

# Varrens — The City of Renewal

Capital of [[Rothas]]. A city for all walks of life — whether you walk the road for knowledge or shadows, you will find like-minded people here. Divided into the **surface city** (several districts) and the **[[Undercity]]** beneath.

**Gates:** North-Western *Flower Gate*, South-Western *Pig Gate*, South-Eastern *Blue Gate* (main)

---

## Districts

### Golden District
The epicenter of trade in Rothas. Countless shops crammed into streets and alleys — the wealthier the shop, the more ornate the front. **Kaln Square** is the heart: a daily market where farmers and traders rent stalls, anchored by the famous **Shrieking Mare** pub and its honey ale. At the center stands a **15-foot granite pillar** covered in ancient runes — one of the last remnants of the Old Empire, largely ignored by passersby.

### Tower District
The diplomatic and bureaucratic heart of the city. Empty streets, decorated mansions, guarded walls. Bureaucratic buildings encircle the inner walls; noble family mansions fill the remainder.

**The Grey Fortress** dominates the district — gray stone, built for function over beauty. Its inner ward contains a flower garden, the main [[White Wardens]] garrison in a fortified watchtower, and the Fortress itself. Inside: a magnificent throne room where **King Kera Rothuss** sits on a richly decorated wooden throne, flanked by 7 silver-armored Wardens carrying white marble-veined blades.

Court advisors:
- **[[Olana Weras]]** (Lawmaster, Knowing Keepers representative) — a long elegant blonde elf in deep blue
- **[[Thrurdir Redfist]]** (Kedar of the White Wardens) — counterbalance to Olana's caution

> [!info] Court Security
> All who approach the dais stand within a **permanent Circle of Truth**. Olana can conjure a Wall of Force; invisible guards in the trusses fire sleep darts at aggressors. A hidden door at the back allows the king to evacuate.

### Grain District
South-east. The smell of fresh bread and pastries lingers in the air. Narrow alleys, small houses, farm warehouses. Houses the **Blue Gate** — main entrance to the city. Travelers can rest at the **Blue House** gatehouse inn.

### Temple District
Home to the **Temple of Enlightenment** and the **[[Knowing Keepers|Academy]]**. The Temple is built around the **[[Life Tree]]** — sprouted from Melora's blood during the War of Deliverance. Those attuned to nature can hear a faint song near the tree: a remnant of the song of creation. The Tree is divided into 8 sections, one per Benevolent deity, with the Raven Queen's ravens nesting in its branches.

> [!danger] Hidden Truth
> The Life Tree is one of the **[[World Anchors|world anchors]]**. When destroyed, the melody will cease.

### District of Iron & The Fiery Pits
The forge district. Heat hits your face on the descent. Cinders float through the air. Home to the city's forges, metalworkers, leatherworkers, and woodworkers.

**Rust** — the city dungeon, a dark building against the city walls. In front sits **The Tower of Feathers**: a completely smooth tower with a gangplank-like protrusion 100 feet up. Public executions are held here — prisoners are led to the gangplank and left; the crowd gambles on how long before they fall or jump.

Two massive metal doors lead down a winding pathway to the **Undercity**, lit by blue-green glowing moss.

### Undercity
The dark cavern system beneath Varrens. Houses the majority of the lower classes and is home to the **[[Black Hand]]**. Stretches far beyond city limits, with an **underground lake** filled by the river from the Tawil Mountains — the city's fresh water source.

No city guards operate here — they withdrew after several riots. The Black Hand fills the authority vacuum.

The only untouched relic: the **[[Sankrit]]** — an ancient pit for offerings, ~20ft in diameter, decorated with carvings of faces in varying levels of anguish. Extends all the way to the Underdark.

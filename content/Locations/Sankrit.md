# The Sankrit

An ancient pit in the [[Varrens]] Undercity, approximately **20 feet in diameter**, decorated with carvings of faces in varying levels of anguish. Used in ancient times as a site for offerings.

The Sankrit extends all the way down to the **Underdark**.

## Current Use
- The [[The Fallen#The Ashen Faithful|Ashen Faithful]] use the Sankrit as their entry point into [[Varrens]] — climbing up from the Underdark below
- Bodies infected by [[The Fallen#The Insects of Disease|the Insects of Disease]] are thrown into the Sankrit, inadvertently preventing discovery of the cause of the mysterious fever deaths

# Ciria

A magocracy founded as a peace agreement between trading companies. Many of its cities are self-controlled — they recognize the Council of Ciria but barely interact beyond trade. Specializes in war magics, in contrast to [[Rothas]]'s physical infantry focus.

## Cities & Locations
- [[Demini]] — Capital
- [[Forest of Orange Wings]] — Central forest, home of [[Kas Aertom]]
- [[Ker'Leg]] — Major mining outpost

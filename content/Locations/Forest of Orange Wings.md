# Forest of Orange Wings

A dark, expansive forest at the center of [[Ciria]], with a mountain range and lake at its core. Named not for the forest itself but for the **blood bugs** that breed here — beautiful orange-red swarms that resemble butterflies from afar.

## Blood Bugs
Nearly harmless most of the year (covering entire trees in color). During **breeding season**: swarms envelope creatures, sting the body, lay an egg, and inject burning acid. The victim falls in paralyzing pain as eggs hatch and larvae feed before crawling out, leaving acid spots. Treatment requires counteracting the acid — leaving equally painful burn scars.

## Lake Innsfjor
Crystal-clear, completely surrounded by mountains at the forest's core — the source of most rivers in the nation. A beautiful view requiring either a dangerous mountain climb or passage through [[Kas Aertom]].

## Cas Aertom
The elven enclave on the southern mountainside, built around the **Mother Tree** where [[Melora]] and [[Myrnes]] once lived. Houses built on scaffolding hung below the canopy. A stream from Innsfjor flows around the Mother Tree. Backed by Myrnes, the inhabitants attack all trespassers — with **specific prejudice against non-elves** — as surrounding settlements have encroached on their forest.

# Mordron

The northern trade city of [[Rothas]], surrounded by water on three sides — ideal for a port. Ruled by **three trade lords**, each living in their own walled palace at the city's center. Overlapping areas of control create constant tension; each raises or lowers their personal tax to nudge trade and curry favor.

## Port Zones
- **North** — Connects to forest; used as shipyard (White Wardens ships to fishing boats)
- **West** — Fishing port for the local population
- **South (main)** — Trade ships; positioned for routes to Lyria or Cirion

# Lyria — The Marble City

Visible from afar for its contrasting white color and the **Scaled Spire** tower at its core. Originally a perfect circle before the [[The Divergence|Divergence]] broke off sections — losing a quarter of its walls but gaining a seaside location, propping up its economy.

## Districts (Concentric Walls)

| Wall | Name | Contents |
|------|------|----------|
| Inner | **Imperial Might** | Scaled Spire (Empress Bahavir's palace), mansions of the most influential noble families |
| Middle | **Holy Protector** | Lesser noble families, stores, markets, wealthier population; the **Temple of the Magnificent** (shining Platinum Dragon statue) |
| Outer | **Glory of the People** | General population; the harbor was awkwardly absorbed here — houses stand directly next to incoming ships |
| Outside walls | — | **Imperial Barracks** — home of the **Platinum Heart** (hand-to-hand elite + **Final Embrace** wyvern brigade) |

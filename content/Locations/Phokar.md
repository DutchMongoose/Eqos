# Phokar Dynasty

One of the few intact remnants from the old world. Located on the continent of **Omas**, sharing a border with the warrior nation of **[[Estaria]]** to the west. Led by **Dragon Empress Bahavir**, whose family traces lineage back to the dragon god [[Bahamut]].

The empress rules from [[Lyria]], overseeing noble families in constant infighting for position — which she is fully aware of and quietly encourages.

## Cities
- Lyria — Capital, the Marble City
- [[Mirrah]] — Mountain border fortress
- [[Miazhar]] — Ancient city, now a criminal haven

# Undercity

The dark cavern system beneath [[Varrens]], accessible via two massive metal doors in the District of Iron, down a winding pathway lit by blue-green glowing moss. Stretches far beyond the city limits above.

## Character
Houses the majority of Varrens' lower classes. No city guards operate here — they withdrew after the **Cavern Riots of 824**, leaving the [[Black Hand]] to fill the authority vacuum as judge and enforcer.

## Key Features
- **Underground lake** — fed by the river from the Tawil Mountains; the city's fresh water source
- **[[Sankrit]]** — ancient offering pit, ~20ft diameter, carved with anguished faces; extends to the Underdark
- **Black Hand dive bar** — a unnamed establishment next to the undercity lake; one of the few places the Hand can be approached

## Connections
The Underdark below connects via the [[Sankrit]] — used by the [[The Fallen#The Ashen Faithful|Ashen Faithful]] to infiltrate Varrens. One underground offshoot from the Tawil Mountains also connects directly to the Undercity, providing a hidden entry from outside the city.

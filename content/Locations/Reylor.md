# Reylor

The **cloud nation** — a dense cloud formation hovering over the Oros region, home to **cloud and storm giants**. Giants interact very little with land creatures, dismissing their limited lifespans and disliking their disrespect for nature.

## Society
Reylor's societal structure is complex and convoluted — outsiders routinely misinterpret the hierarchy and accidentally insult giants. Their internal politics are opaque to most ground-dwellers.

## Notable Residents
- **[[Golner]]** — a storm giant who left to join the [[Knowing Keepers|Illustrious Five]]; formerly the lorekeeper for Reylor
- **[[Trull]]** — visited on a failed expedition before the [[The Divergence|Divergence]]

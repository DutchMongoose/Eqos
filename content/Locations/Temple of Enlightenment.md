# Temple of Enlightenment

The grand temple in the [[Varrens]] Temple District, built around the [[Life Tree]] — which sprouted from blood shed by [[Melora]] during the War of Deliverance.

## Layout
The temple is divided into **8 sections**, one dedicated to each of the Benevolent deities. Each section is themed around its deity's aspect.

## The Life Tree
At the heart of the temple grows the [[Life Tree]]. Those attuned to nature can hear a faint song near the tree — a remnant of the song of creation.

> [!danger] Hidden Truth
> The Life Tree is one of the [[World Anchors|world anchors]]. When destroyed, the melody will permanently cease.

The [[Raven Queen]]'s ravens nest in the branches of the Life Tree, deeply associating the temple with death.

## The Academy
Also located in the Temple District: the [[Knowing Keepers|Academy]] — headquartered in the same district but a separate building.

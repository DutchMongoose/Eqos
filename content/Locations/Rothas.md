# Rothas

A relatively new kingdom on the continent of **Ocura**, stretching from frozen tundras in the north to noble alliances in the south. An often-contested eastern border with [[Ciria]]. Founded from the ruins of [[Fior]] by the noble House of [[House Rothuss|Rothuss]], defended using the ancient **Gates of Puolust**.

**Current ruler:** King **Kera Rothuss** — a well-liked human with slightly graying hair.

The population is concentrated around several large cities and dwarven strongholds, with scattered villages throughout the wilds. Some nomadic groups and tribes still roam the lands.

## Cities
- [[Varrens]] — Capital
- [[Gira]] — Vassal dwarven city
- [[Tharium]] — Eastern border city, Warden training
- [[Mordron]] — Northern trade port

## Teleportation Hazards
Due to the lingering effects of the [[The Divergence|Divergence]], teleportation magic within Rothas is unreliable. Roll d100 when casting a teleportation spell:

| d100 | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 01–10 | Dimensional rupture — 6d8 psychic damage, all targets feebleminded (DC 16 INT save every 24hr) |
| 11–20 | Explosive force — all within 10ft take 8d10 force damage; structures take double |
| 21–40 | Absorbed into the astral — spell fails; all spell slots of same level lost until next long rest |
| 41–60 | Rampant teleport — 6d8 force damage, creatures sent random direction (1d10: 1–8 clockwise, 9 down, 10 up) |
| 61–70 | Spell slot expended; nothing happens |
| 71–80 | Memory loss — all targets lose last day of memory; not teleported |
| 81–90 | Successful teleport but all spell slots and hit dice lost until next long rest |
| 91–100 | Functions as intended |

# Demini — The Sapphire City

Visible from afar for its **distinct blue glow on the horizon** — gas preserves under the city fuel blue streetlights, reflecting beautifully in the water. The city bridges both sides of the **Turtle's Favor River** estuary, with long well-made passages connecting the richer northern side (Bears district) and the newer south (Wolves district). Both sides have their own harbour. Trade flows with surrounding kingdoms and with **Ker'Ala**, a nearby dwarven settlement.

Ciria is a **magocracy** — ruled by mages, with the Council of Niêve at the top.

## The Cirian Moon
The **Council of Niêve** — a select group of mages hiding behind bureaucracy — discovered a **gem containing an air djinn** while searching for artifacts to counter rising Rothas tensions. Using this, they pulled their prominent fortress from the ground into the sky. **The Cirian Moon** now serves as a mobile fortress, carrying forces and intimidating attackers.

## The Talons
The criminal organisation operating in Demini's underbelly. Known to guard valuable artifacts.

## Justice System
Every crime has a distinct fine. Once held accountable, the guilty choose:
1. **Pay the fine** (advantageous for the rich)
2. **Take the Plunge** — the fine in coins is bound to the criminal's chest; they swim across the river. Bystanders gamble on survival, time, and breath counts. Survive: acquitted, coins returned. Drown: money retrieved, family held accountable for fines.

> [!warning] Systemic Debt
> Workers are held accountable for all grievances — a family can be fined for equipment lost when a worker dies in a mine collapse. This creates generational debt cycles and forced labor.

# Tharium

Situated on a narrow hill with cliff-edged lakes on both sides — a natural land bridge between steady ground. Home to the **[[White Wardens]]** training grounds (north side) and a key eastern strategic asset in border struggles with [[Ciria]].

**Commander:** **Lady Myela** — a retired orcish Compinaar. Tasked with the city proper but spends much of her time at the garrison watching recruits train. Frequents the fighting ring to stay sharp.

## City Character
Narrow streets and tall buildings create near-permanent shade. Population is mostly retired soldiers — shops cater accordingly. Ornamental weapons are abundant.

**The Smelly Blade** — a gimmick sword shop. Swords sold as a surprise for the experience. Range includes: a butter blade that never expires, a sword whose handle gives a random daily scent. The owner's father made a fey pact so enthralled with weapons that it left the owner with **sword blades for legs**.

## The Crater

Outside the city lies a **massive crater**. Beneath a cave at its base rests a skeleton — the remains of someone sacrificed as a deliberate target point to guide a meteor strike. The scale of the impact suggests it was not an accident.

## The Lakes
Both lakes are dark and filled with dangerous beasts; similar water snakes in each suggest an underground connection. The eastern lake has a small island (~200ft diameter) with a dense patch of forest.

**Liadell** — Hidden within that forest patch, home of the **Liadon elves** — a reclusive group who helped combat a necromantic infection of nature just after the Divergence. Average visitors walk right through the forest. Those deemed interesting instead enter Liadell.

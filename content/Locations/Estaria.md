# Estaria

A warrior nation of many clans — stationary and nomadic — all pledged to the **Tirr**, who lives in **Elmrim**. When a Tirr dies, each clan proposes a candidate; consensus usually takes a week, often shortened by combats of pride. Each clan is self-sufficient (hunting or fishing). They worship a **nameless towering being** of great strength and endless knowledge.

Shares a border with [[Phokar]] to the east.

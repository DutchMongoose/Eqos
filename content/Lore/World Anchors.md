# World Anchors

Dimensional shackles created by the [[Knowing Keepers]] shortly after the [[The Divergence|Divergence]] to keep the planes of existence separate — preventing gods, demons, and other extra-planar creatures from entering the material plane.

## Known Anchor Locations
| Anchor | Location | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Main Anchor** | [[The Eye|Aexidor's demiplane]] ([[Grey Woods]]) | The tree with greenish glow and red leaves; most heavily protected |
| **Varrens Anchor** | [[Life Tree]], [[Temple of Enlightenment]], [[Varrens]] | Hidden in plain sight as a holy tree; next Fallen target |
| Others | Various — placed at locations significant to the Keepers | Can be positioned anywhere in your campaign |

## Current Threat
[[The Fallen]] are systematically destroying the anchors. The more that are removed, the easier dimensional travel becomes and the more extra-planar incursions occur. When **all anchors are destroyed**, every creature banished by the Divergence — including all the gods — can return to the material plane.

> [!danger] Imminent
> The Fallen will attack the **Varrens Anchor** during the **mid-winter festival**. *See [[History Overview]] and [[DM Tools]].*

## Effect of Destruction
Each destroyed anchor weakens the planar boundary. Otherworldly influences begin seeping through — manageable at first, but escalating toward full incursions as more are lost. The [[Deck of Truths#The 20 Cards|the Mystery card]] card of the [[Deck of Truths]] depicts 7 chalk arrows on the door keyhole stonework — one per world anchor.

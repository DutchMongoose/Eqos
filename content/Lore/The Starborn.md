# The Starborn

Corrupted souls transported to the [[Far Realms]] during the [[The Divergence|Divergence]], now servants of the [[Banished Ones]] and ground forces for [[The Fallen]].

## Origins
Over centuries in the Far Realms, their minds and bodies were warped by the alien environment:
- **Weaker minds** devolved into **lesser star spawn** — grunt soldiers
- **Stronger minds** retained conscience and gained powers over mind and space

## Arrival Method
Once enough [[World Anchors]] are destroyed, the Starborn will arrive on the material plane via **meteorite**. They manifest as a **dark mass of worms** that infect dead hosts, assuming their form while retaining the ability to dissolve back into a formless mass.

## Strategy
After arrival, they travel to major cities and begin **preaching the truth of the Divergence** to the masses — undermining the [[Knowing Keepers]]' version of history.

*See [[The Fallen]] for full context.*

# The Divergence

The cataclysmic event that split the singular continent of Eqos into many, destroyed the city of [[Fior]], and sealed the [[Banished Ones|banished gods]] away from the material plane. The exact cause remains unknown — all records from [[Fior]] at the time were lost in the event itself.

## Competing Accounts

| Nation / Group | Their Account |
|---|---|
| [[Phokar]] | The dragon god [[Bahamut]] engulfed the world in flames and flew into the sun, causing earthquakes |
| [[Ciria]] | The birth of a new star broke through the ground and rose as the all-seeing eye |
| Various tribes | The Day of Retribution — ancient spirits purified the world to start anew |
| [[Knowing Keepers]] of [[Rothas]] | The Benevolent Gods warred with the [[Banished Ones]] and sealed them to protect their subjects |
| [[The Fallen]] | All gods were sealed away equally — making them equal parts victim |

## Consequences
- The single continent split into many; families and cities destroyed at rupture lines
- [[Fior]] reduced to rubble
- The [[Banished Ones]] sealed away from the material plane
- [[World Anchors]] created by the [[Knowing Keepers]] to maintain separation of planes
- Teleportation magic became unreliable throughout [[Rothas]]
- [[House Rothuss]] built a fortress at the ruins of Fior, founding [[Rothas]]

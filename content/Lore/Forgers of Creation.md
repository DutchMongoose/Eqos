# Forgers of Creation

Six primordial minds that gained consciousness after a unique collision between the elemental planes. Walking the border between dreams and the waking world, the world of Eqos flowed from their thoughts — water, plants, mountains, and eventually life itself.

## Nature
They exist almost in a dream state, shaping reality from their thoughts as one shapes a lucid dream. Once content with their creation, each retreated to a **resting place** to sleep properly — with the intention to return.

## Current State
Their bodies sit motionless in **stasis on massive thrones** across Eqos. Their resting places are largely unknown, though frost giants in the **Northern Wastes** have awakened near one such site. Their power can still be tapped — or they could potentially be awakened and swayed.

> [!info] Six Forgers
> Their individual names, resting places, and specific roles in shaping the world are yet to be fully documented — a potential thread for exploration.

## Known Throne Locations
One Forger's throne has been located in the region of **[[Mordron]]**. The approach passes through a **garden of bones** and is guarded by [[Rha'zoc]].

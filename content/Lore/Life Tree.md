# The Life Tree

A large tree at the heart of the [[Temple of Enlightenment]] in [[Varrens]], around which the entire temple was built. Sprouted from **blood shed by [[Melora]]** during the War of Deliverance.

Those closely attuned to nature can hear a **faint tune** playing near the tree — a remnant of the song of creation.

## Hidden Nature
> [!danger] Classified — Known only to a select group within the [[Knowing Keepers]]
> The Life Tree is one of the **[[World Anchors|world anchors]]**. When destroyed, the melody will permanently cease and the planar boundary at this location will be broken.

## The Raven Queen's Ravens
The [[Raven Queen]]'s ravens nest in the branches of the Life Tree, deeply associating the temple with death in the eyes of worshippers.

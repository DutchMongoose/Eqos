# Banished Ones

The gods sealed away during the [[The Divergence|Divergence]] — cast into the [[Far Realms]] and other outer planes, cut off from the material plane by the [[World Anchors]] created by the [[Knowing Keepers]].

## Their Goal
The Banished Ones are manipulating [[The Fallen]] into destroying the [[World Anchors]], which would allow them to re-enter the material plane. Each god has their own agenda once freed.

## Their Influence
Despite being sealed away, the Banished Ones can still reach out to mortals:
- [[Narja]] opened a mental eye in [[Nherdur]], guiding him toward the truth of the Divergence
- The Banished Ones empower [[Rhazi]]'s spells and granted her a third eye
- [[Myrnes]] has swayed the elves of [[Kas Aertom]] to attack outsiders
- [[Kalryn]] has contacted the doppelgangers ([[The Shapeless]]) to serve his purpose

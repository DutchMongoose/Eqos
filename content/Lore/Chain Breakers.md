# Chain Breakers

> [!warning] Classified
> Their very existence is officially dismissed as a bedtime tale. Students who discuss them are temporarily banned from the [[Knowing Keepers|Academy]].

A covert operations group within the [[Knowing Keepers]]: a hybrid of mages and rogues with access to **ancient magics predating the [[The Divergence|Divergence]]**. Overseen by [[Faethana]] (the second of the [[Knowing Keepers|Illustrious Five]]).

## Members
Approximately 30 assassin-mages, many hidden in plain sight in neighbouring empires.

## Runes of Power
Each member bears **runes of power** branded into their arms (not merely tattooed — objects are inserted into the skin). These tattoos:
- Empower spells
- Grant access to various ancient magics when fully trained

## Known Members / Former Members
- [[Rhazi]] — recruited but defected to [[The Fallen]]; self-inflicted scars where she removed her runes

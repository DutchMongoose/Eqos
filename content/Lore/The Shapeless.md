# The Shapeless — Children of Kalryn

Doppelgangers, long cast out of society, have been contacted by [[Kalryn]] to serve his divine purpose. They infiltrate factions and cities by copying identities and reading the thoughts of those they imitate.

## Power Tiers

| Level | Abilities | Tell |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Base | Takes form; cannot eat or mimic body temperature; **shudders** (loses shape for 1 min, once/day) | Kalryn's mark is large (cow-spot sized) |
| Second | No eating or temperature issues; still shudders | Mark is smaller |
| Highest | Perfect copy; no shudder; very rare | Mark can be as small as a birthmark |

## Detection
Observing a potential Shapeless for an **entire day** resolves doubt — the shudder will manifest at least once for lower tiers. The highest tier requires either catching the mark or magical detection.

## Kalryn's Mark
When any doppelganger is killed, **Kalryn's sign briefly appears on their neck** before the body withers away — a brand left on all his children.

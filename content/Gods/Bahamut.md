# Bahamut — The Platinum Dragon

The power behind law and dragonkind. Figurehead of the [[Phokar]], whose rulers claim divine lineage to Bahamut as their god-given right to rule.

## Depiction
An ancient dragon covered in glimmering platinum scales, with a **massive diamond at the center of his chest** — a wound from his final mortal battle.

## Before the Divergence
A **silver dragonborn paladin** wearing equally shiny armor. Wielded a longsword engraved with draconic writing describing the prophecy of Bahamut. During the War of Deliverance, he sustained a fatal chest wound — which became the gem at his divine form's center.

> [!warning] The Ruse
> Bahamut was never a true dragon — he had silver scales, not platinum. The devoted of Phokar follow a fabrication: their leaders **polish their scales** to appear platinum in imitation.

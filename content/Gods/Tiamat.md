# Tiamat — The Winged Doom

The only god who was purely evil even before ascending. A dragonborn warlord who became an archdevil of the Nine Hells.

## Before the Divergence
Commanded a troop of bandits and outcasts, raiding villages and outposts — leaving few survivors. Many of her crew wore **dragon masks** to imitate their dragonborn leader. Known for gathering local leaders from target settlements and toasting them alive with her fire breath.

## After the Divergence
Ascended directly to the **Nine Hells**, gaining the full power of her ancestral blood: the body of an ancient dragon with **four additional chromatic heads**, each under her full control. Quickly seized command of her layer of Hell and now commands **legions of devils**. Awaits an opportunity to escape and destroy her rival [[Bahamut]].

# Sanci — The Devourer

Once a culinary savant and joyful soul. Now a mindless avatar of hunger.

## Before the Divergence
A gifted cook who imbued creations with magical effects — ranging from minor to laughable. Famous incidents include a meat pie that left an entire noble house flying around for a day, and cookies that made guards make only animal sounds for hours (timed so local children could enjoy the spectacle). Called herself the **Guardian of her Gardens**, cultivating and testing ingredients from all lands.

## After the Divergence
The cataclysm left her retreating into her mind — food, eating, hunger, devour — until only this remained. The Far Realms slowly transformed her into the embodiment of hunger itself. Now she roams the **[[Far Realms]]** as a **ginormous snake with a diameter of 100 feet and immeasurable length**, often visible in several places at once as dimension shards overlap, cross, and reform. Fortunately, she is too large and too distracted to notice individual souls.

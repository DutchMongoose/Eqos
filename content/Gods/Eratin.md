# Eratin

God of coins, gold, and deception. His philosophy: every person has three faces — the public face shown to the world, the family face for close relationships, and the true face rarely revealed. To succeed in his cornerstones, one must know someone's masks and act upon them.

## Depiction
A slender figure holding **three masks** in front of his face.

## Before the Divergence
A master dwarven thief — short, stout, and swift — who stole for the thrill, not the treasure. He robbed banks and manors simply to force the wealthy to upgrade their defenses and vaults.

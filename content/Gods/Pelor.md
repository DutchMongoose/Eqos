# Pelor

God of light and the common folk. Even the smallest hamlet or harbor has at least a shrine dedicated to him. His religion stands for power to the people and strongly opposes dark magics, including necromancy.

## Depiction
A heavily armored form with a **bright light as a head**.

## Worship & Sites
- People pray for good harvests, fair weather, and safe haven
- Sits in **Celestia**, where he discusses matters like the Blood War with the angelic population — pays little attention to mortal pleas directly
- The **Shrine of Promises** (open-air, on a mountaintop near [[Gira]]) is the only material location where angelic attention can be gained
- A **small mountain ridge east of [[Varrens]]** marks where Pelor was thrown back in combat during the War of Deliverance, leaving a large imprint in the western face

## Before the Divergence
The most formidable fighter in his orc clan, wielding a **granite longsword** and a **massive iron-rimmed door shield**. He and his army fought against the armies of [[Fior]] at the start of the Divergence. He and **Ker** transformed into giant forms and clashed while their armies rushed between their legs.

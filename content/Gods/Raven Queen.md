# The Raven Queen

*"The finality of death is something that comes for all eventually."*

The mistress of death and guide between life and the afterlife. Ever-silent in her communication — most pray to her to stay away rather than seek her favor. Ravens are deeply associated with death because of her, as they help collect souls of the departed and carry them to her realm.

## Depiction
A fragile figure with long dark hair, clad completely in black with a **white porcelain mask** — blank in emotion, decorated only with black stripes extending downward from the eyes and mouth. Large feathered wings spread from her back.

## Worship
Any site of prayer is surrounded by an unkindness of ravens. In the [[Temple of Enlightenment]] in [[Varrens]], her ravens take home in the branches of the [[Life Tree]]. Powerful souls may require multiple ravens to collect them — scores of ravens attending funerals have been recorded throughout history.

## Before the Divergence
Known simply as **Ista**, a small, moon-pale human sorcerer with a lifelong fascination with death. As a child, she accidentally activated a runic circle in an abandoned graveyard — her friends disappeared into purple flame and reappeared as skeletons, their flesh melted away. The spirits within those skeletons set her on her path to power.

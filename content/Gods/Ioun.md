# Ioun

Lady of knowledge and strategy. Patron deity of the [[Knowing Keepers]], whose symbol incorporates her third-eye emblem.

## Depiction
A tall woman with a **third eye placed vertically on her forehead** — her symbol and the basis for the Knowing Keepers' insignia. Does not hold a specific dimension but makes her home in **Celestia**.

## Before the Divergence
An elf with light long hair, often dressed in deep blue, with an eye tattoo on her forehead. Grew up in the elven enclave now known as the **Forest of Memory**, a secluded area home to many elder elves and an impressive collection of ancient knowledge.

# Kalryn — The Many-Faced

A theater performer who became the god of deception and shapeshifting — and progenitor of the doppelgangers.

## Before the Divergence
Loved plays involving disguises. Could briefly change appearances when required. At times performed all characters in a play himself, just because he could. Performed for the less fortunate, and at times collaborated with [[Sanci]] to bring special magical effects to the stage.

## After the Divergence
Became the many-faced god, possessing the countless faces of souls that tried to sway him. Spawned the **doppelgangers** — creatures that deceive and imitate identities for their own ends. Each doppelganger still bears his mark: when one is killed, **Kalryn's sign briefly appears on their neck** before they wither away.

# Narja — The Pale Star

An astronomer who became a literal star, visible across all planes of existence.

## Before the Divergence
Enthralled by the skies, she recorded star movements and noticed patterns — certain positions coincided with incursions from other dimensions. First to propose that other dimensions are detectable as blips in the sky. Her work gave rise to prediction methods including the **[[Deck of Truths]]**.

## After the Divergence
Now embodies a **star visible from every plane**, always in the same position in the sky. Those aligned with Narja find that the Deck of Truths provides truths that shed light on their troubles.

*See also: [[Nherdur]] — received visions from Narja guiding him toward the [[Banished Ones|banished gods]].*

# Myrnes — The Eye that Sees

Sister of [[Melora]]. Where Melora governs plants and nature, Myrnes cares for all creatures that fill those lands.

## Before the Divergence
Lived in a large expansive forest near her sister, unbeknownst to Melora, watching over creatures of all kinds. Despite caring for creatures, she despised humanity — watching them clear forest for agriculture and housing, driving animals away. She placed **wards** around encroaching settlements to make people forget where they were and why they came.

## After the Divergence
Her protective nature only intensified. Now taking the form of a **massive eye surrounded by smaller eyes**, Myrnes watches and sees all dealings — nudging the dice in favor of creatures wherever possible. Has reignited her feud with humanity by swaying the reclusive elves of the **Forest of Orange Wings** ([[Kas Aertom]]) to her side.

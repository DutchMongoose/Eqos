# Trull — The Sorrowed One

A driven scholar who absorbed knowledge at rapid speeds and sought ever more, at any cost. Repelled those around him with his arrogance; never minded his solitude.

## Before the Divergence
A researcher who pushed into dark corners of magical study. Had just returned from a failed expedition to [[Reylor]] when the Divergence occurred, leaving him ever-doubting whether to sign an infernal contract he had received. The disruption of his work left his mind in perpetual unrest.

## After the Divergence
Schemes and broods, having surpassed even the general goal of lichdom. **Envies the unreachable knowledge** of the [[Knowing Keepers]] above all else. Relaxation brings him no comfort — he mulls endlessly over all he learned in mortal life.

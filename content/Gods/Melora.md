# Melora — The Wildmother

Nature embodied, loved by all. Plants, lands, and the sea all fall under her domain.

## Worship
Followers prefer **natural shapes of worship** — temples are rarely statues. Her temples are in distant natural locations (making pilgrimages difficult), but any shrine gains her attention: nearby plant life grows healthier and faster.

One of the few gods who communicates through **avatars** — a body of vines and twigs with flowers for eyes.

The **[[Life Tree]]** in the [[Temple of Enlightenment]] sprouted from blood Melora shed during the War of Deliverance.

## Before the Divergence
Waist-length auburn hair, short even for an elf, with flowers always growing in her hair and small animals following her around. Her backpack was an open bird's nest the birds returned to each year. She lived in the woods for large portions of the year and could transform into a squirrel to live among them.

*Sister of [[Myrnes]].*

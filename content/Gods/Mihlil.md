# Mihlil

Goddess of music and peace — the two forever entwined. Her aspects are the vices: music, drink, and art.

## Depiction
Always shown in a resting position, eyes closed, with a harp in her hands.

## Before the Divergence
A poet and artist who traveled through all nations performing for establishments of all statuses — as likely to play along the road for travelers and beggars as for wealthy parties. She never wrote her work down, so most of it was lost with time. Some distant bards claim to have discovered her songs, though false claims are common.

# Ker — The King in Yellow

A horned warrior and former judge who stood for justice and repentance. Now grown vindictive in his unrightful banishment.

## Before the Divergence
The fiercest warrior of his time, wielding a **dark stone double-bladed axe**. Based in **Fyrik**, a small city near the borders of several kingdoms, he acted as a judge — calm of mind, judging each case separately, earning wide respect. During the War of Deliverance he died defending the gates of [[Fior]] against Pelor's attacking army. A temple dedicated to him stood on the pre-Divergence continent, later lost in the Divergence along with Fior itself.

## After the Divergence
Found his former community burned up in the Divergence — no one left to reach out to. This left him sorrowful and vindictive of his unrightful banishment. Now watches from his **massive stone throne**, longing to return to the material plane and make matters right.

Visions recovered from the ruins of Fior depict him as the **King in Yellow** — armored, seated on his throne, presiding over a grief-stricken court. These visions are connected to the [[Chain Breakers]].
